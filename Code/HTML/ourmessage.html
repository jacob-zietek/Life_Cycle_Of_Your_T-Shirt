Our Message

We aim to educate people on the sourcing of their clothing, because fast fashion is one of the main contributors to global pollution and human rights violations. We do not want to discourage the use of overseas manufacturers because the business does create a lot of jobs in nations with much higher poverty rates than the United States, rather, we want to expose unsustainable and unethical practices so that people in America can put pressure on private corporations. Those who need the most help are those who have the least amount of power. If a group of underpaid workers attempt to collectively bargain for higher wages, they would be fired immediately because there are too many quick replacements for unskilled laborers. It is up to us to demand change for those who cannot demand it for themselves.
